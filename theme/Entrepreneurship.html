Online MBA in Entrepreneurship

The Online MBA in Entrepreneurship is designed to create and nurture the next generation of business innovators and startup founders. This 2-year program combines core business administration concepts with practical entrepreneurial training, equipping students with the skills to launch and grow successful ventures.

  • UGC-recognized MBA degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering ideation, validation, funding, and scaling
  • Practical learning through business plan development and pitch sessions
  • Mentorship from successful entrepreneurs and industry experts
  • Access to startup incubation and funding networks

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Dual specialization options available
  • Action-based learning through real venture creation
  • Startup funding pitch competitions
  • Access to investor networks
  • Option to incubate your startup during the program

Career Pathways

  • Startup Founder/Co-founder
  • Business Consultant
  • Innovation Manager
  • Venture Capital Analyst
  • Business Development Manager
  • Family Business Successor
  • Corporate Entrepreneur (Intrapreneur)

Program Curriculum

The MBA in Entrepreneurship curriculum blends business fundamentals with entrepreneurial skill development:

  • Financial Management

Entrepreneurship Specialization Subjects

  • Design Thinking for Entrepreneurs
  • Family Business Management

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • Entrepreneurial mindset and passion for innovation
  • No age limit for admission
  • Existing business owners/family business successors welcome

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th, and Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / Migration Certificate
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voter ID)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Business plan or venture idea (optional)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive:

  • MBA degree with specialization in Entrepreneurship
  • UGC-recognized and NAAC accredited degree
  • Digital mark sheet and certificate
  • Certificate of Venture Creation (for students who launch businesses)
  • Lifetime access to entrepreneur alumni network

Note: The degree certificate will not mention "Online" mode, making it equivalent to a regular MBA degree.
