Online MBA in Banking Management

The Online MBA in Banking Management is designed to develop professionals who can excel in the dynamic banking sector. This 2-year program combines core business administration concepts with specialized banking knowledge, preparing students for leadership roles in financial institutions.

  • UGC-recognized MBA degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering banking operations, risk management, and financial services
  • Case-study based learning with real-world banking scenarios
  • Flexible online learning with industry-relevant projects
  • Career-focused specialization with high demand in banking sector

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Dual specialization options available
  • Industry-aligned curriculum with banking focus
  • Live projects and case studies from banking sector
  • Online proctored examinations
  • Capstone project in final semester

Career Opportunities

  • Bank Manager
  • Relationship Manager
  • Credit Analyst
  • Risk Manager
  • Investment Banker
  • Wealth Manager
  • Financial Consultant

Program Curriculum

The MBA in Banking Management curriculum is designed to provide both foundational business knowledge and specialized banking expertise:

Core MBA Subjects
  • Principles of Management
  • Managerial Economics
  • Accounting for Managers
  • Business Research Methods
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Financial Management
  • Operations Management
  • Human Resource Management
Banking Specialization Subjects
  • Banking and Financial Services
  • Risk Management in Banking
  • Retail and Corporate Banking
  • Investment Banking
  • International Banking
  • Financial Markets and Institutions
  • Credit Management
  • Digital Banking and Fintech

Special Features

Practical Learning

Case studies, banking simulations, and live projects for hands-on experience

Industry Focus

Special emphasis on contemporary banking practices and regulations

Banking Experts

Guidance from banking professionals and financial experts

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • Work experience in banking/finance preferred but not mandatory
  • No age limit for admission
  • Valid score in national/state level MBA entrance exam (if applicable)

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th, and Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / Migration Certificate
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voter ID)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Work experience certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive:

  • MBA degree with specialization in Banking Management
  • UGC-recognized and NAAC accredited degree
  • Digital mark sheet and certificate
  • Alumni status with networking opportunities
  • Placement assistance for eligible students

Note: The degree certificate will not mention "Online" mode, making it equivalent to a regular MBA degree.
