Online MBA in Investment Banking & Wealth Management

The Online MBA in Investment Banking & Wealth Management is designed to create finance professionals who can excel in high-stakes financial environments. This 2-year program combines core business administration concepts with specialized knowledge in capital markets, portfolio management, and financial advisory services, preparing students for prestigious roles in investment banks, wealth management firms, and financial institutions.

  • UGC-recognized MBA degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering securities analysis, M&A, and private banking
  • Practical training on financial modeling and valuation techniques
  • Case-study based learning with real-world deal scenarios
  • Career-focused specialization with placement assistance in top financial firms

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Dual specialization options available
  • Industry-aligned curriculum with Wall Street practices
  • Hands-on financial modeling exercises
  • Training on Bloomberg Terminal (virtual access)
  • Capstone project analyzing live deals and portfolios

Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive:

  • MBA degree with specialization in Investment Banking & Wealth Management
  • UGC-recognized and NAAC accredited degree
  • Digital mark sheet and certificate

Frequently Asked Questions

While both are finance specializations, they serve different purposes:
  • Investment Banking focuses on corporate finance activities like M&A, IPOs, and capital raising for businesses
  • Wealth Management deals with personal finance, helping individuals grow and protect their assets
This MBA covers both areas, giving you flexibility to pursue either career path or roles that combine both skill sets.
You'll master industry-standard modeling techniques including:
  • Three-statement financial models (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
  • Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) valuation
  • Leveraged Buyout (LBO) models
  • Merger models and accretion/dilution analysis
  • Comparable company and precedent transaction analysis
  • Portfolio optimization models
  • Risk assessment and scenario analysis
These skills are essential for both investment banking and wealth management roles.
This MBA provides excellent preparation for the CFA and other finance certifications by:
  • Covering 70% of CFA Level I syllabus content
  • Teaching core finance concepts tested in these exams
  • Providing practice with quantitative methods and analysis
  • Offering exam strategy sessions
  • Giving access to supplemental study materials
Many students pursue these certifications concurrently or immediately after completing the MBA.
We provide multiple networking channels including:
  • Virtual meetups with alumni in finance roles
  • Guest lectures from investment bankers and wealth managers
  • Access to finance professional associations
  • Participation in virtual finance conferences
  • Mentorship programs with senior finance professionals
  • LinkedIn groups and discussion forums
These connections often lead to internships, job opportunities, and valuable industry insights.
