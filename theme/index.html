About Edutantra

Founded in 2010, Edutantra has helped over 5,000 students successfully study abroad in countries like USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, and more. Our team of experienced counselors provides end-to-end support from university selection to post-arrival assistance.

We maintain partnerships with 200+ top-ranked universities worldwide and have a 100% visa success rate. Our personalized approach ensures each student gets tailored guidance based on their academic background, career aspirations, and budget.
