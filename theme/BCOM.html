Online Bachelor of Commerce (BCom)

The Online BCom program is designed to provide students with fundamental knowledge of commerce principles and their application in real-world scenarios. This 3-year undergraduate degree program is perfect for students who want to pursue careers in accounting, finance, banking, or business.

  • UGC-recognized degree from NAAC A+ accredited universities
  • Flexible online learning with 24/7 access to study materials
  • Industry-relevant curriculum with practical applications
  • Affordable fee structure with easy installment options
  • Multiple specialization options to match career goals

Program Highlights

Program Structure

The Online BCom program is divided into 6 semesters (3 years) with a comprehensive curriculum covering all essential aspects of commerce education:

Semester Core Subjects Electives
Semester 1-2
  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Mathematics
  • Principles of Management
  • Business Communication
  • Computer Applications
Semester 3-4
  • Corporate Accounting
  • Business Laws
  • Cost Accounting
  • Income Tax
  • E-Commerce
Semester 5-6
  • Auditing
  • Financial Management
  • Specialization Subjects
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Project Work

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum qualification: 10+2 or equivalent from any recognized board
  • No minimum percentage requirement (varies by university)
  • No age limit for admission
  • Working professionals can apply
  • Students appearing for final exams can apply provisionally

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / School Leaving Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card / Passport as ID proof
  • Passport size photographs
  • Work experience certificate (if applicable)
