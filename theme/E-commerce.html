Online MBA in E-Commerce & Marketing Management

The Online MBA in E-Commerce & Marketing Management is designed to create digital-first business leaders who can drive growth in the booming e-commerce sector. This 2-year program combines traditional marketing principles with cutting-edge digital commerce strategies, preparing students for leadership roles in the digital marketplace.

  • UGC-recognized MBA degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering e-commerce platforms, digital marketing, and online consumer behavior
  • Practical training on Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon Seller Central, and other e-commerce tools
  • Flexible online learning with live e-commerce projects
  • 100% placement assistance with top e-commerce companies

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Dual specialization options available
  • Industry-aligned curriculum with digital commerce focus
  • Hands-on training on e-commerce platforms and tools
  • Live projects with real e-commerce businesses
  • Option for certification in Digital Marketing

Career Opportunities

  • E-Commerce Manager
  • Digital Marketing Manager
  • Marketplace Specialist (Amazon/Flipkart)
  • SEO/SEM Specialist
  • Social Media Marketing Manager
  • E-Commerce Entrepreneur
  • Product Manager (Digital)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • Basic computer and internet literacy required
  • No age limit for admission
  • Valid score in national/state level MBA entrance exam (if applicable)

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th, and Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / Migration Certificate
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voter ID)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Work experience certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive:

  • MBA degree with specialization in E-Commerce & Marketing Management
  • UGC-recognized and NAAC accredited degree
  • Digital mark sheet and certificate
  • Option for Google Digital Marketing Certification (additional fee)
  • Placement assistance with top e-commerce companies

Note: The degree certificate will not mention "Online" mode, making it equivalent to a regular MBA degree.
