Online MA in Journalism & Mass Communication

The Online MA in Journalism & Mass Communication (MAJMC) is a comprehensive postgraduate program designed to equip students with advanced skills in media, journalism, and communication. This 2-year program covers all aspects of modern media practices.

  • UGC-recognized degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Specializations in Digital Media, PR, and Broadcast Journalism
  • Practical training through case studies and media projects
  • Flexible online learning with industry-relevant curriculum
  • No age limit for admission

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Comprehensive coverage of media theories and practices
  • Specializations in emerging media fields
  • Recognized by UGC and DEB
  • Practical assignments and media projects
  • Dissertation in final semester

Career Opportunities

  • Journalist (Print/Electronic/Digital)
  • Public Relations Officer
  • Content Writer/Editor
  • Media Planner
  • Broadcast Producer
  • Social Media Manager
  • Corporate Communications Specialist

Program Structure

The Online MA in Journalism & Mass Communication program is divided into 4 semesters (2 years) with a comprehensive curriculum:

Semester Core Subjects Electives
Semester 1
  • Introduction to Mass Communication
  • Reporting & Editing
  • Media Laws & Ethics
  • Photojournalism
  • Development Communication
Semester 2
  • Media Research Methods
  • Advertising & Public Relations
  • Radio Journalism
  • Television Production
Semester 3
  • Digital Media & Online Journalism
  • Media Management
  • Corporate Communication
  • Documentary Filmmaking
Semester 4
  • Dissertation/Media Project
  • Specialized Reporting
  • Media & Society

Available Specializations

Digital Media Journalism

Public Relations

Broadcast Journalism

  • Radio Programming
  • Anchoring & Reporting
  • Broadcast Writing

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • No specific stream requirement in bachelor's
  • No age limit for admission
  • Basic computer and internet literacy

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th & Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / School Leaving Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card / Passport as ID proof
  • Passport size photographs
  • Portfolio (if available, for media-related work)
