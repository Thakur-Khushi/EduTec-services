Online MBA in Foreign Trade

The Online MBA in Foreign Trade is designed to create global business leaders who can navigate the complexities of international trade. This 2-year program combines core business administration concepts with specialized knowledge in global trade policies, cross-border transactions, and international marketing, preparing students for careers in export-import management and global business expansion.

  • UGC-recognized MBA degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering international trade laws, documentation, and logistics
  • Practical training on EXIM procedures and global trade platforms
  • Case-study based learning with real-world international trade scenarios
  • Career-focused specialization with high demand in global trade sector

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Dual specialization options available
  • Industry-aligned curriculum with global trade focus
  • Training on international trade documentation
  • Virtual simulations of export-import transactions
  • Capstone project analyzing global market entry strategies

Career Opportunities

  • Customs Compliance Officer
  • Foreign Trade Analyst
  • International Logistics Manager
  • Trade Finance Specialist

Program Curriculum

The MBA in Foreign Trade curriculum blends international business fundamentals with specialized trade knowledge:

Core MBA Subjects
  • Principles of Management
  • Managerial Economics
  • Accounting for Managers
  • Business Research Methods
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Financial Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Operations Management
Foreign Trade Specialization Subjects
  • International Business Environment
  • Export-Import Procedures & Documentation
  • Global Marketing Strategies
  • International Trade Laws & WTO Regulations
  • Foreign Exchange Management
  • Cross-Cultural Business Communication
  • International Logistics & Supply Chain
  • Trade Finance & Risk Management

Global Trade Focus Areas

Trade Documentation

Mastery of EXIM documents like Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin

Trade Finance

Letters of Credit, Bank Guarantees, Export Credit

Market Analysis

Identifying global opportunities and competitive positioning

Logistics

Incoterms, Shipping, Customs Clearance

Compliance

Understanding trade regulations and tariffs

Trade Analytics

Analyzing global trade data and trends

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • Interest in international business and global markets
  • No age limit for admission
  • Valid score in national/state level MBA entrance exam (if applicable)

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th, and Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / Migration Certificate
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voter ID)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Work experience certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive:

  • MBA degree with specialization in Foreign Trade
  • UGC-recognized and NAAC accredited degree
  • Digital mark sheet and certificate
  • Option for EXIM certification (additional fee)
  • Placement assistance with export-import firms

Note: The degree certificate will not mention "Online" mode, making it equivalent to a regular MBA degree.
