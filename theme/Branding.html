Online MBA in Branding & Advertising

The Online MBA in Branding & Advertising is designed to develop marketing professionals who can create powerful brands and effective advertising campaigns. This 2-year program combines core business administration concepts with specialized knowledge in brand management and creative advertising.

  • UGC-recognized MBA degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering brand strategy, consumer behavior, and advertising management
  • Case-study based learning with real-world branding challenges
  • Flexible online learning with creative marketing projects
  • High-demand specialization with excellent career prospects in advertising and marketing

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Dual specialization options available
  • Industry-aligned curriculum with branding focus
  • Creative advertising and brand strategy projects
  • Online proctored examinations
  • Capstone project in brand management

Career Opportunities

  • Brand Manager
  • Advertising Manager
  • Marketing Communications Manager
  • Creative Director
  • Media Planner
  • Digital Marketing Manager
  • Market Research Analyst

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • Work experience preferred but not required
  • No age limit for admission
  • Valid score in national/state level MBA entrance exam (if applicable)

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th, and Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / Migration Certificate
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voter ID)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Work experience certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive:

  • MBA degree with specialization in Branding & Advertising
  • UGC-recognized and NAAC accredited degree
  • Digital mark sheet and certificate
  • Alumni status with networking opportunities
  • Placement assistance for eligible students

Note: The degree certificate will not mention "Online" mode, making it equivalent to a regular MBA degree.
