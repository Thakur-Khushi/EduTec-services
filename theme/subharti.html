About SVSU Distance Education

Swami Vivekanand Subharti University (SVSU) is a private university established in 2008 and located in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and approved by the Distance Education Bureau (DEB). SVSU offers various distance education programs to provide flexible learning opportunities for students who cannot attend regular classes.

The university's distance education programs are designed to maintain the same academic standards as their regular counterparts. SVSU Distance Education provides comprehensive study materials, online resources, and a robust evaluation system to ensure quality education for all its students.

Key Highlights

  • Recognized by UGC and DEB
  • Approved by AICTE, BCI, PCI, NCTE
  • NAAC Accredited
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified
  • Member of Association of Indian Universities (AIU)

SVSU's distance education programs follow a learner-centric approach with a perfect blend of theoretical knowledge and practical applications. The university provides printed study materials, e-learning resources, and regular academic support to ensure students receive a comprehensive learning experience.

Detailed Admission Process

The admission process for SVSU Distance Education programs is designed to be simple and student-friendly. Here are the complete steps:

Step-by-Step Admission Procedure

  1. Program Selection: Choose your desired program from the available options
  2. Eligibility Check: Ensure you meet all eligibility criteria for the selected program
  3. Application Form:
    • Obtain the application form from the university website or authorized centers
    • Fill out the form with accurate personal and academic details
  4. Document Submission:
    • Attach all required documents (scanned copies for online application)
    • Ensure documents are clear and legible
  5. Application Fee Payment:
    • Pay the non-refundable application fee through available payment modes
    • Online payment options include credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI
  6. Form Submission:
    • Submit the completed application form before the deadline
    • Keep a copy of the submitted form for future reference
  7. Admission Confirmation:
    • Receive admission confirmation via email/SMS within 7-10 working days
    • Check your enrollment details in the confirmation
  8. Fee Payment:
    • Pay the program fee as per the installment plan
    • Payment receipt will be generated for your records
  9. Study Material Dispatch:
    • Study materials will be dispatched to your registered address
    • Tracking details will be shared for your reference

Required Documents Checklist

  • Academic Documents:
    • 10th mark sheet and certificate (for all programs)
    • 12th mark sheet and certificate (for UG programs)
    • Graduation mark sheets and degree certificate (for PG programs)
    • Provisional certificate if final degree not available
  • Identity Proof:
    • Aadhar Card (mandatory)
    • Passport/Driving License/Voter ID (any one)
  • Photographs:
    • Recent passport size color photographs (5 copies)
    • Digital copy for online application
  • Signature:
    • Scanned copy of signature
    • Physical signature on hard copy forms
  • Additional Documents (if applicable):
    • Caste certificate (SC/ST/OBC candidates)
    • Income certificate for fee concession
    • Gap year affidavit (if applicable)
    • Migration certificate (for students from other universities)
Important Notes:
  • All documents must be self-attested
  • Original documents may be required for verification at the time of examination
  • Name in all documents should match exactly with identity proof
  • In case of name discrepancy, submit relevant name change documents

Examination System & Evaluation

SVSU follows a comprehensive examination system for its distance education programs to ensure proper evaluation of students' knowledge and skills.

Examination Pattern

  • Semester System: Most programs follow a semester pattern with exams conducted twice a year
  • Term-end Examinations: Held at designated centers across India
  • Question Paper Pattern:
    • Theory papers: 70 marks
    • Internal assessment: 30 marks (based on assignments)
    • Practical examinations for relevant programs
  • Passing Criteria:
    • Minimum 40% marks required in each paper (35% for SC/ST candidates)
    • Separate passing criteria for theory and practical components

Examination Process

  1. Exam Form Submission:
    • Fill the examination form before the deadline
    • Pay the examination fee as applicable
  2. Admit Card:
    • Download from student portal 15 days before exams
    • Mandatory to carry to exam center with valid ID proof
  3. Exam Center Allocation:
    • Based on student's preferred location
    • Changes allowed with prior request and valid reason
  4. Result Declaration:
    • Typically within 45 days of exam completion
    • Available on university website and student portal

Frequently Asked Questions
