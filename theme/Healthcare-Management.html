Online MBA in Healthcare Management

The Online MBA in Healthcare Management is designed to create business leaders who can navigate the complex healthcare industry. This 2-year program combines core business administration concepts with specialized healthcare knowledge, preparing students for leadership roles in hospitals, pharmaceutical companies, health insurance, and public health organizations.

  • UGC-recognized MBA degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering hospital administration, healthcare laws, and health informatics
  • Case-study based learning with real-world healthcare scenarios
  • Flexible online learning with industry-relevant projects
  • Career-focused specialization with high demand in growing healthcare sector

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Dual specialization options available
  • Industry-aligned curriculum with healthcare focus
  • Healthcare simulations and case studies
  • Online proctored examinations
  • Capstone project solving real healthcare management challenges

Career Opportunities

  • Hospital Administrator
  • Healthcare Consultant
  • Pharmaceutical Project Manager
  • Health Insurance Manager
  • Medical Practice Manager
  • Public Health Administrator
  • Healthcare Analytics Manager

Program Curriculum

The MBA in Healthcare Management curriculum is designed to provide both business and healthcare expertise:

Core MBA Subjects
  • Principles of Management
  • Managerial Economics
  • Accounting for Managers
  • Business Research Methods
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Financial Management
  • Operations Management
  • Human Resource Management
Healthcare Specialization Subjects
  • Hospital Administration
  • Healthcare Laws and Ethics
  • Health Economics and Policy
  • Healthcare Quality Management
  • Health Informatics
  • Pharmaceutical Management
  • Healthcare Marketing
  • Public Health Systems

Healthcare Management Focus Areas

Hospital Operations

Patient care systems, facility management, and service delivery

Healthcare Analytics

Data-driven decision making for improved patient outcomes

Quality & Compliance

Accreditation standards and regulatory requirements

Healthcare Finance

Reimbursement systems and cost containment strategies

Patient Experience

Service excellence and patient satisfaction measurement

Digital Health

Telemedicine and healthcare technology solutions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • Healthcare professionals preferred but not mandatory
  • No age limit for admission
  • Valid score in national/state level MBA entrance exam (if applicable)

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th, and Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / Migration Certificate
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voter ID)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Work experience certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive:

  • MBA degree with specialization in Healthcare Management
  • UGC-recognized and NAAC accredited degree
  • Digital mark sheet and certificate
  • Option for Healthcare Quality Certification (additional fee)
  • Placement assistance with healthcare organizations

Note: The degree certificate will not mention "Online" mode, making it equivalent to a regular MBA degree.
