Online MBA in Business Analytics & Intelligence

The Online MBA in Business Analytics & Intelligence is designed to create data-driven business leaders who can transform complex data into actionable insights. This 2-year program combines core business administration concepts with advanced analytics training, preparing students for high-demand roles in the data economy.

  • UGC-recognized MBA degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering data analysis, predictive modeling, and business intelligence
  • Hands-on learning with industry-standard tools (Python, R, Tableau, Power BI)
  • Flexible online learning with real-world analytics projects
  • Career-focused specialization with 100% placement assistance

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Dual specialization options available
  • Industry-aligned curriculum with analytics focus
  • Practical training on analytics tools and platforms
  • Capstone analytics project with real business data
  • Option for IBM certification in data analytics

Career Opportunities

  • Business Analyst
  • Data Analyst
  • Analytics Manager
  • Business Intelligence Analyst
  • Marketing Analyst
  • Financial Analyst

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • Basic quantitative skills preferred
  • No age limit for admission
  • Valid score in national/state level MBA entrance exam (if applicable)

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th, and Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / Migration Certificate
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voter ID)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Work experience certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive:

  • MBA degree with specialization in Business Analytics & Intelligence
  • UGC-recognized and NAAC accredited degree
  • Digital mark sheet and certificate
  • Option for IBM Data Analytics Professional Certificate (additional fee)
  • Placement assistance for eligible students

Note: The degree certificate will not mention "Online" mode, making it equivalent to a regular MBA degree.

Frequently Asked Questions

No prior programming experience is required. The program is designed to teach analytics from fundamentals to advanced levels. We start with basic concepts and gradually introduce programming (Python/R) and analytics tools. However, students with quantitative backgrounds (commerce, science, engineering) may find some concepts easier to grasp initially.
You'll work on real-world analytics projects including:
  • Customer segmentation and profiling
  • Sales forecasting and predictive modeling
  • Marketing campaign analysis
  • Financial risk assessment models
  • Operations optimization projects
  • Sentiment analysis of customer feedback
  • Capstone project solving business problems with data
These projects will form a significant part of your analytics portfolio.
While technical data science programs focus heavily on algorithms and programming, our MBA in Business Analytics emphasizes:
  • Business context for analytics applications
  • Decision-making using data insights
  • Strategic implementation of analytics
  • Communication of findings to non-technical stakeholders
  • Integration of analytics with business functions
It's designed for professionals who want to bridge the gap between technical teams and business leadership.

  • Networking with alumni in analytics roles
  • Career counseling sessions
