Online MBA in Mass Communication & Media Management

This 2-year UGC-recognized program combines business administration with specialized training in media, entertainment, and digital communication. Designed for future media leaders, it covers traditional mass communication alongside emerging trends like OTT platforms and digital content monetization.

  • NAAC-accredited MBA degree with dual specialization options
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering media planning, entertainment economics, and digital strategies
  • Hands-on training with media business software and analytics tools
  • Industry projects with production houses and media organizations
  • Special focus on regional language media markets and global trends

Program Highlights

  • Media business models and entertainment economics
  • Film/TV production management modules
  • Audience analytics and consumption pattern studies
  • Workshops on scriptwriting and production budgeting
  • Case studies from Bollywood and regional entertainment
  • Capstone project developing complete media business plans

Career Opportunities
