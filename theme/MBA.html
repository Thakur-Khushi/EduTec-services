Online MBA Program

The Online MBA is a postgraduate program designed for professionals who want to enhance their business management skills and advance their careers. This 2-year program provides comprehensive training in all functional areas of business with options for specialization.

  • UGC-recognized degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Flexible online learning with 24/7 access to study materials
  • Multiple specialization options to match career goals
  • Affordable fee structure with easy installment options
  • No age limit for admission

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering all business functions
  • Specialization options in high-demand areas
  • Recognized by UGC and DEB
  • Online examinations and case study projects
  • Industry-relevant curriculum

Program Structure

The Online MBA program is divided into 4 semesters (2 years) with a comprehensive curriculum covering all business functions:

Semester Core Subjects Electives
Semester 1
  • Management Principles
  • Managerial Economics
  • Accounting for Managers
  • Business Communication
  • Organizational Behavior
Semester 2
  • Marketing Management
  • Financial Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Business Research Methods
  • Operations Management
Semester 3
  • Strategic Management
  • Specialization Course 1
  • Specialization Course 2
  • Entrepreneurship
  • International Business
Semester 4
  • Specialization Course 3
  • Specialization Course 4
  • Project Work
  • Business Ethics
  • Corporate Governance

Special Features of the Program

Case Study Approach

The program emphasizes real-world business scenarios through case studies from Indian and global companies.

Industry Projects

Students work on live projects with companies to gain practical experience and build professional networks.

Leadership Development

The curriculum includes modules on leadership, decision-making, and strategic thinking.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • Work experience preferred but not mandatory
  • No age limit for admission
  • Basic computer literacy for online learning

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th & Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / School Leaving Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card / Passport as ID proof
  • Passport size photographs
  • Work experience certificate (if applicable)
