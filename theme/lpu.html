About LPU Distance Education

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university located in Phagwara, Punjab, India. Established in 2005, LPU is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and offers a wide range of distance education programs to cater to the needs of students who prefer flexible learning options.

LPU's distance education programs are designed to provide quality education with a focus on practical knowledge and skill development. The university offers comprehensive study materials, online resources, and a supportive learning environment to ensure student success.

Key Highlights

  • UGC recognized and approved programs
  • NAAC accredited with an 'A' grade
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified
  • Wide range of programs across various disciplines
  • Strong industry connections for better placement opportunities

LPU's distance education approach emphasizes a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, ensuring that students are well-prepared for their careers.

Key Features of LPU Distance Learning

Detailed Admission Process

The admission process for LPU Distance Education programs is straightforward and designed to assist students:

Step-by-Step Admission Procedure

  1. Program Selection: Choose your desired program from the available options.
  2. Eligibility Check: Ensure you meet all eligibility criteria for the selected program.
  3. Application Form:
    • Obtain the application form from the university website.
    • Fill out the form with accurate personal and academic details.
  4. Document Submission:
    • Attach all required documents.
    • Ensure documents are clear and legible.
  5. Application Fee Payment:
    • Pay the non-refundable application fee through available payment modes.
  6. Form Submission:
    • Submit the completed application form before the deadline.
  7. Admission Confirmation:
    • Receive admission confirmation via email/SMS.
  8. Fee Payment:
    • Pay the program fee as per the installment plan.
  9. Study Material Dispatch:
    • Study materials will be dispatched to your registered address.

Required Documents Checklist

  • Academic Documents:
    • 10th mark sheet and certificate.
    • 12th mark sheet and certificate.
    • Graduation mark sheets and degree certificate.
  • Identity Proof:
    • Aadhar Card (mandatory).
    • Passport/Driving License/Voter ID.
  • Photographs:
    • Recent passport size color photographs (5 copies).
Important Notes:
  • All documents must be self-attested.
  • Original documents may be required for verification.

Examination System & Evaluation

LPU follows a structured examination system for its distance education programs:

Examination Pattern

  • Semester System: Most programs follow a semester pattern.
  • Term-end Examinations: Held at designated centers across India.
  • Question Paper Pattern:
    • Theory papers: 70 marks.
    • Internal assessment: 30 marks.
  • Passing Criteria:
    • Minimum 40% marks required in each paper.

Examination Process

  1. Exam Form Submission:
    • Fill the examination form before the deadline.
  2. Admit Card:
    • Download from student portal before exams.
  3. Exam Center Allocation:
    • Based on student's preferred location.
  4. Result Declaration:
    • Typically within 45 days of exam completion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is LPU Distance Education valid for government jobs?

A: Yes, LPU is UGC-recognized and its degrees are valid for government jobs where distance education is accepted.

Q: What is the duration of the MBA program?

A: The MBA program through distance mode is of 2 years duration, divided into 4 semesters.

Q: Are there any entrance exams for admission?

A: No, admissions are based on eligibility criteria and there are no entrance exams.
