Online MBA in Information Technology

The Online MBA in Information Technology is designed to create tech-savvy business leaders who can bridge the gap between IT and management. This 2-year program combines core business administration concepts with specialized IT knowledge, preparing students for leadership roles at the intersection of business and technology.

  • UGC-recognized MBA degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering IT strategy, digital transformation, and tech management
  • Practical learning through IT projects and case studies
  • Flexible online learning with industry-relevant assignments
  • Career-focused specialization with high demand in tech-driven organizations

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Dual specialization options available
  • Industry-aligned curriculum with IT-business focus
  • Hands-on IT management exercises
  • Online proctored examinations
  • Capstone project solving real business technology challenges

Career Opportunities

  • IT Project Manager
  • Business Technology Analyst
  • IT Consultant
  • Digital Transformation Manager
  • Product Manager (Tech)
  • Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
  • IT Strategy Manager

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • Basic computer literacy required
  • No age limit for admission
  • Valid score in national/state level MBA entrance exam (if applicable)

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th, and Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / Migration Certificate
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voter ID)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Work experience certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive:

  • MBA degree with specialization in Information Technology
  • UGC-recognized and NAAC accredited degree
  • Digital mark sheet and certificate
  • Option for IT Management Certification (additional fee)
  • Placement assistance with technology companies

Note: The degree certificate will not mention "Online" mode, making it equivalent to a regular MBA degree.
