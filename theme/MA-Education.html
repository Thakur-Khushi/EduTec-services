Online MA in Education

The Online MA in Education is a postgraduate program designed for educators and professionals who want to enhance their knowledge and skills in the field of education. This 2-year program provides advanced training in educational theory, research, and practice.

  • UGC-recognized degree from NAAC accredited universities
  • Flexible online learning with 24/7 access to study materials
  • Specialized curriculum focusing on contemporary educational practices
  • Affordable fee structure with easy installment options
  • No age limit for admission

Program Highlights

  • 2-year postgraduate degree program (4 semesters)
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering educational theory and practice
  • Specialization in contemporary educational methodologies
  • Recognized by UGC and DEB
  • Online examinations and dissertation
  • Practical assignments and research projects

Career Opportunities

  • School Administrator/Principal
  • Education Consultant
  • Curriculum Developer
  • Educational Researcher
  • Teacher Educator
  • Instructional Coordinator
  • Education Policy Analyst

Program Structure

The Online MA in Education program is divided into 4 semesters (2 years) with a comprehensive curriculum covering all aspects of education:

Semester Core Subjects Electives
Semester 1
  • Philosophical Foundations of Education
  • Psychological Foundations of Education
  • Sociological Foundations of Education
  • Educational Technology
  • Comparative Education
Semester 2
  • Research Methodology in Education
  • Curriculum Development
  • Educational Measurement and Evaluation
  • Guidance and Counseling
Semester 3
  • Teacher Education
  • Educational Management
  • Special Education
  • Adult and Continuing Education
Semester 4
  • Dissertation
  • Contemporary Issues in Education
  • Environmental Education

Special Features of the Program

Practical Orientation

The program emphasizes practical application of educational theories through case studies, projects, and field-based assignments.

Research Focus

Students undertake a research project in their area of interest, developing skills in educational research and data analysis.

Technology Integration

The curriculum includes training in educational technology and digital tools for teaching and learning.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 50% marks in graduation (45% for reserved categories)
  • Teaching experience preferred but not mandatory
  • No age limit for admission
  • Basic computer literacy for online learning

Documents Required

  • 10th, 12th & Graduation Mark sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate / School Leaving Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card / Passport as ID proof
  • Passport size photographs
  • Experience certificate (if applicable)
